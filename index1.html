Update on The Release of NECO Results

The National Examination Council (NECO) has reported that more than one million candidates have registered and will be participating in the ongoing 2023 Senior School Certificate Examination.

The Registrar of NECO, Prof. Dantani Wushishi, made this announcement in Jos, Plateau State on Friday while overseeing the progress of the examination in various schools.

As per a statement released by the Council's spokesperson, Azeez Sani, out of the registered candidates, 601,074 are male while 584,814 are female.

The statement also quoted the NECO head expressing contentment with the overall conduct of the 2023 SSCE based on reports collected from different parts of the country.

"The Registrar stated that the 2023 SSCE, set to conclude next week, has been the most well-organized in recent years. Prof. Wushishi shared that several measures have been implemented to curb examination malpractice, and these measures have started showing positive outcomes," the statement mentioned.

"The Registrar, after overseeing the examination at Airforce Military School, King and Queens Academy, and Emmanuel International College, all located in Jos, expressed his approval of the examination's conduct in these institutions," the statement added.
